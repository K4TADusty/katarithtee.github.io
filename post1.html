In this post, I'll share how I created a fast, minimal blog using static HTML, CSS, and a bit of JavaScript for interactivity. The goal was to keep it simple, accessible, and quick to load.

Key decisions included using a dark theme with green accents, optimizing images, and avoiding heavy frameworks. The result is a blog that loads in under 1 second on most connections.
