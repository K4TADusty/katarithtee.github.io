Why I prefer simple static HTML over heavy frameworks

Written by Katarithtee on 22 Jul 2025
Education

Arguments for simplicity: maintenance, speed, and accessibility when your content is mostly text.

Image caption here

For blogs and content sites, static HTML offers unbeatable performance and reliability. This post discusses the benefits of avoiding complex frameworks, focusing on SEO, load times, and ease of hosting.

While frameworks have their place, for many projects, plain HTML with CSS and minimal JS is sufficient and superior.
